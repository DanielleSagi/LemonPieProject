How to prepare

In a bowl:

  1. Crush the fenugreek into a powder (in a food processor).
  2. Melt the butter and add to the bowl.
  3. Add cinnamon, sugar and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix until you get the texture of slightly wet sand.
  5. Shape the mold and put in the oven for 10 minutes at 100-150 degrees Celsius.

Let's move on to preparing the filling:

  1. In a separate bowl, place the yolks, and then the condensed milk.
  2. Mix them well.
  3. Add the juice and lemon zest and mix everything until you get a uniform texture.
  4. Remove the pastry crust from the oven and let it cool.
  5. When it's cold, pour the filling we prepared into it, and flatten it with gentle taps.
  6. Put it in the oven at 180 degrees Celsius for half an hour.
  7. But if you can and are not in a hurry, it's better to leave it in the oven for much longer at a much lower temperature.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it dance if it dances, like jelly.If it dances, let it sit for a little longer.
  9. After it's ready, put it in the refrigerator for a while and that's it.
Enjoy!


Tips:

  • If we leave it in the oven at a lower temperature and for longer, the pie will be more stable and less likely to fall apart when cut.
  • It is highly recommended to make meringue and quickly drizzle it over the pie and burn its edges with a burner, after baking for a more beautiful appearance.
  • It is recommended to choose a pan without straight sides, as this makes it difficult to remove the pie. The more sloping it is, the better.
